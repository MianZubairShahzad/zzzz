Pakistan’s Trade Deficit Narrows 12% in FY24

According to data released by the Pakistan Bureau of Statistics (PBS), the country’s trade deficit was down 12.3 percent in FY24 compared to deficit of $27.474 billion reported in FY23. The country’s exports during FY24 stood at $30.64 billion, up 10.5 percent over exports of $27.724 billion in FY23. Imports remained largely unchanged, coming in at $54.734 billion in FY24 compared to $55.198 billion in FY23.
